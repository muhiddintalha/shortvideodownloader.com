Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: we don't collect, store, or sell your personal data. No accounts, no cookies, no tracking. Video files are processed temporarily and deleted within 1 hour.

1. What we collect

We collect nothing that identifies you personally. When you use the service:

  • The video URL you submit is sent to our server to process your request. It is not logged or stored after processing.
  • The downloaded video file is stored in an isolated temporary directory on our server for the duration of the transfer, then automatically deleted — always within 1 hour.
  • Standard web server logs may record your IP address and request metadata (timestamp, HTTP status code). These logs exist for security and abuse-prevention purposes only, are not linked to the content you download, and are rotated regularly.

2. Cookies and tracking

We use no cookies, no analytics scripts, no advertising trackers, and no third-party scripts of any kind. There are no session cookies, no persistent identifiers, and no fingerprinting techniques.

3. Third-party platforms

When you request a video, our server makes a request to the relevant platform (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc.) on your behalf. Those platforms have their own privacy policies and may log the request from our server's IP address. We do not share any information about you with those platforms.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party. We have no data to share.

5. Data retention

Temporary video files are deleted within 1 hour of creation. Server logs are rotated regularly. We retain no long-term data tied to individual users or requests.

6. Security

All connections are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Video files are processed in isolated temporary directories. Our backend enforces rate limiting and strict input validation to prevent abuse.

7. Children's privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
